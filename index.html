Chapter I: The First Henlo

It tried to say hello. It came out as henlo. Poku did not apologise. The internet decided this was correct. A greeting was born. A cult followed shortly after, mostly because nobody could stop saying it back.

Chapter II: The Wander

Poku roamed. Poku seeded. Poku carried a bag and occasionally a flamethrower. Nobody asked about the flamethrower. This was considered the correct response. Poku's silence on the matter remains canon.

Chapter III: Ooga Booga

The community found each other. Art was made. Sites were built. People said henlo to complete strangers and felt something. Poku nodded once, sat down in some fire, and disappeared into late 2025. This page is the evidence.
